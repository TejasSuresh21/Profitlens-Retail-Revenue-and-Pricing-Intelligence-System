## 🎯 Business Problem
Revenue is growing, but **profitability is inconsistent**. Leadership needed answers to:  

1. Which categories & regions are structurally unprofitable?  
2. How much revenue is lost due to discounting?  
3. What are the fastest actions to recover profit?

## 🔑 Key Insights & Recommendations

| Insight | Action | Impact |
|--------|--------|--------|
| ₹0.61M revenue at risk (Furniture & Office Supplies) | Track Revenue at Risk KPI; prioritize high-loss categories | Protects margins without reducing revenue |
| Discounts >30% create ₹468.7K loss-making revenue | Enforce discount caps & approvals beyond break-even | Reduces structural losses |
| High revenue ≠ high profit (Technology) | Focus on profit per order & margin efficiency | Improves overall profitability |
| East & Central regions drive most losses | Apply targeted pricing & discount controls | Maximize recoverable profit |
| ₹43.28K recoverable profit in Office Supplies | Correct top loss segments first; monitor via KPIs | Quick, actionable profit recovery |



---

##  Conclusion
Revenue growth alone **does not guarantee profitability**. Profit leakage is primarily due to:  
- Aggressive discounting  
- Low-margin categories  
- Regional concentration of losses  

**Next Steps:**  
1. Correct top-loss segments first (Office Supplies & Furniture)  
2. Enforce disciplined discount governance  
3. Embed margin-centric KPIs for ongoing monitoring
